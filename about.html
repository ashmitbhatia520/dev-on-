The Challenges is to recognize fraudulent credit card transactions so that the customers of credit card companies are not charged for items that they did not purchase



Main challenges involved in credit card fraud detection are:

  • Enormous Data is processed every day and the model build must be fast enough to respond to the scam in time.
  • Imbalanced Data i.e most of the transactions (99.8%)are not fraudlant which makes it really hard for detecting the fraudlant ones.
  • Data availability as the data is mostly private.
  • Misclassified Data can be another major issue, as not every fraudlant transaction is caught and reported.
  • Adapted techniques used against the model by the scammers.

How our website use to tackle from these problems?

  • we use IAM technology to protect card details.
  • we use two factor authentication according to which when a payment will be done by user a notification will reflect on our website in which either we want to accept it or decline the payment or if he /she doesn't click on that link for few minute the payment will automatically declined.
